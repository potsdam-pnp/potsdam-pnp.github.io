Initiative Tracker Privacy Policy Version 1.0

This privacy policy is only valid for the version of the Initiative Tracker app, that is distributed via the Google Play Store. For other distribution channels, check the Initiative Tracker website. We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our app.

When the application is updated, new data collections might be added, that will be described in an updated privacy policy. Note, that the source code application is also available on GitHub. You can check the source code to verify what data is collected by us.

Information we collect

We don't collect any personal information. The app can transmit technical information when the app crashes. This may include technical information such as the device type, Android version as well as stack traces and log information.

Note, that some information may also be collected by Google when you install this application via the Google Play Store. Please check the relevant privacy policy to get more information about Google's data collection.

How we use information

The information is used to fix bugs and improve stability of the application.

Third party services

No third-party services are used.
