Initiative Tracker Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is only valid for the version of the Initiative Tracker app, that is distributed via the Google Play Store. For other distribution channels, check the Initiative Tracker website. We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our app.

Information we collect

We don't collect any personal information. The app can transmit technical information when the app crashes. This may include technical information such as the device type and Android version.

How we use information

The information is used to fix bugs and improve stability of the application.

Third party services

No third-party services are used.
